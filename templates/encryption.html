Directions to decrypt the encrypted part of the flag

Step 1. XOR with 0x5a to get an SHA1 hash

Step 2. Find the string that produces the SHA1 hash

Step 3. The string you get in step 2 is encypted using a caesar cipher with left shift 7.

Decrypt it and then enclose it in CTF_SDaT{...!} to get the flag.

The encrypted part of the flag (Don't copy this from the HTML code, it will contain wrong characters): 
        c9<co>;9>hhl>9<ni>;iml9>8l988;mjiohkbchk
